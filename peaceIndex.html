Peace by Piece

Be a Peace of the Puzzle Child’s Play’s new peace initiative begins with an interactive workshop that challenges children to focus on ways that they can change the world through positive actions. Stories poems created at these workshops will be showcased in a new touring staged production, Peace by Piece.
